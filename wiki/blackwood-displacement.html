Subject Silt / Scalpel Convergence
Priority High - Saturday Story

Field Report

This entry documents the convergence of the Blackwood Silt and the Pearl-Handled Scalpel. It is a singular record of the Silt manifesting outside of designated containment following a localized power failure in the Lenistry.

The moisture is not condensation, but a biological displacement. When left in proximity to the unsecured Scalpel, the Silt began to occupy the physical space of the treatment room, forming a wet silhouette on the massage table—a study of the past in transit between physical states.

"The history is there, heavy and unseen, felt only in the stillness of the room as the past looks back."
